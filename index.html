MAIN CONTENT TYPES
Since Instagram is a visual platform, all of the content you post will showcase your brand’s personality. You’re not just posting a new image or a new video or Story – you’re placing a new piece in a much bigger puzzle.
Specific types of posts that you can create for your Instagram business channel:

-Behind the scenes: photos, short videos, Stories, events you’re attending and other visuals.
-Brand videos and Stories.
-Product videos, images and Stories.
-Educational/how-to types of posts.
-Useful tips.
-Quotes: images with quotes can perform very well.
-IGTV – a great opportunity to explore long-form vertical video to reach your audience.

Instagram is not the same for a long time.
If before people only filmed "for a social network", now the opposite is true. Random, blurry, life photos gain more views and likes than specially prepared photo sessions. We think that people have begun to accept everyday life as an art and have the ability to see beauty in small things. If you think so too then welcome to our site.
